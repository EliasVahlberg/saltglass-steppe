# Lore-Driven Content Expansion Summary

**Date**: 2026-01-02  
**Process**: Iterative lore analysis and content creation  
**Iterations**: 10 major expansion cycles  

## Overview

This document summarizes a comprehensive content expansion process that systematically analyzed the rich narrative lore of Saltglass Steppe and translated it into concrete game content. The process involved 10 major iterations, each focusing on different aspects of the lore and adding corresponding gameplay elements.

## Expansion Process

### Methodology
1. **Lore Analysis**: Examined narrative documents to identify content gaps
2. **Content Creation**: Added missing creatures, NPCs, items, and structures
3. **Thematic Integration**: Ensured all additions fit the established tone and world
4. **Iterative Refinement**: Each iteration built upon previous additions
5. **Comprehensive Coverage**: Addressed multiple lore domains systematically

### Content Sources Analyzed
- The Quantum Bestiary (creature lore)
- The Dramatis Personae (key character profiles)  
- The Lithopedia of Glass (material science)
- The Schism Wars (faction history)
- The Atlas of Glass (geographical locations)
- The Psychic Codex (ability systems)
- The Heliograph Expedition (cosmic storyline)
- Multiple other lore documents
