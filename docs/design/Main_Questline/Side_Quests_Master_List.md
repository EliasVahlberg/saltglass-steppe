# Side Quests Master List

*A compendium of 25+ side quests to flesh out the Saltglass Steppe, categorized by faction and region.*

## I. Tutorial & Survival (The Outskirts)

1.  **The First Spark**
    *   **Giver:** Old Man Silas (Starting Camp)
    *   **Context:** The camp's main UV-lamp is flickering. Shadows are encroaching.
    *   **Objective:** Scavenge 3 `Copper Wire` and 1 `Glass Bulb` from the nearby ruins.
    *   **Reward:** `Rusty Pipe` (Weapon), +5 XP.

2.  **Thirst for Clarity**
    *   **Giver:** Mara the Water-Merchant
    *   **Context:** The local water filter is clogged with silt.
    *   **Objective:** Kill 3 `Silt-Slugs` at the riverbank and collect `Filter Mesh`.
    *   **Reward:** `Purified Water` x3, +10 Scrip.

3.  **The Rat King's Crown**
    *   **Giver:** Camp Guard
    *   **Context:** A giant `Glass-Rat` is stealing food supplies.
    *   **Objective:** Track and kill the `Alpha Glass-Rat` in the sewers.
    *   **Reward:** `Rat-Tooth Dagger`, +50 XP.

---

## II. The Iron Covenant (Magma-Glass Caverns)

4.  **Scrap for the Forge**
    *   **Giver:** Quartermaster Kael
    *   **Context:** The Covenant needs raw metal to reinforce the bunker doors.
    *   **Objective:** Collect 10 `Scrap Metal` from `Automaton Wrecks`.
    *   **Reward:** `Iron Plating` (Armor Upgrade), +10 Iron Rep.

5.  **Silence the Signal**
    *   **Giver:** Lieutenant Vance
    *   **Context:** A rogue radio tower is broadcasting "Monk propaganda" (actually just music).
    *   **Objective:** Destroy the `Radio Transmitter` at Sector 4.
    *   **Reward:** `Frag Grenade` x2, +15 Iron Rep, -5 Monk Rep.

6.  **The Traitor's Log**
    *   **Giver:** Security Chief
    *   **Context:** A soldier deserted last night. Find proof of where he went.
    *   **Objective:** Find `Datapad: Private Miller` in the Wastes.
    *   **Reward:** Choice: Turn it in (+Iron Rep) or Burn it (+Humanity).

7.  **Heavy Metal**
    *   **Giver:** Smithy Durgan (if friendly)
    *   **Context:** Durgan wants to build a masterpiece weapon but needs `Titanium-Glass`.
    *   **Objective:** Defeat a `Heavy Loader Bot` and loot its chassis.
    *   **Reward:** `Pneumatic Hammer` (Heavy Weapon).

8.  **Bunker Defense**
    *   **Giver:** Automated Alert System
    *   **Context:** A swarm of `Shard-Spiders` is breaching the lower levels.
    *   **Objective:** Survive 5 waves of enemies in the entry hall.
    *   **Reward:** `Covenant Medkit` x5, +20 Iron Rep.

---

## III. The Mirror Monks (The Glass Gardens)

9.  **Light Calibration**
    *   **Giver:** Acolyte Lira
    *   **Context:** The mirrors in the Meditation Garden are misaligned.
    *   **Objective:** Rotate 3 `Prism Towers` to direct light to the central statue.
    *   **Reward:** `Focus Lens` (Accessory, +Accuracy), +10 Monk Rep.

10. **Pilgrimage of Pain**
    *   **Giver:** The Penitent One
    *   **Context:** To understand the glass, one must walk upon it barefoot (metaphorically).
    *   **Objective:** Walk 100 tiles in the `Sharp-Glass Zone` without healing.
    *   **Reward:** `Trait: Calloused Feet` (Immune to floor hazards).

11. **Shard Gathering**
    *   **Giver:** Keeper of the Archives
    *   **Context:** The Monks need `Memory Shards` to record history.
    *   **Objective:** Collect 5 `Blue Shards` from `Crystalline Echoes`.
    *   **Reward:** `Scroll of Insight` (+Intelligence), +15 Monk Rep.

12. **The Heretic**
    *   **Giver:** Inquisitor Sol
    *   **Context:** A monk has started preaching that the storms are bad (heresy!).
    *   **Objective:** Find Brother Thomas. Choice: Convince him to recant or help him escape.
    *   **Reward:** `Mirror Shield` or `Smuggler's Map`.

13. **Crystal Gardening**
    *   **Giver:** The Gardener
    *   **Context:** The `Singing Lilies` are being choked by `Iron-Weeds`.
    *   **Objective:** Remove 5 `Iron-Weeds` without damaging the flowers.
    *   **Reward:** `Lily Extract` (Full Heal Potion).

---

## IV. The Synthesis Seekers (Nexus Plateau)

14. **Data Retrieval**
    *   **Giver:** Researcher Aris
    *   **Context:** An old server farm was exposed by the last storm.
    *   **Objective:** Download `Server Logs` before the battery dies (Time Limit: 50 turns).
    *   **Reward:** `Data-Spike` (Hacking Tool), +10 Synthesis Rep.

15. **Flora Analysis**
    *   **Giver:** Botanist Ren
    *   **Context:** A new type of moss is growing on the robots.
    *   **Objective:** Scan/Collect `Techno-Moss` samples.
    *   **Reward:** `Bio-Scanner` (Reveals enemy stats).

16. **Diplomatic Immunity**
    *   **Giver:** Saint Matthias
    *   **Context:** An Iron Covenant patrol and a Monk procession are about to fight.
    *   **Objective:** Diffuse the situation using Speech checks (requires Charisma > 15).
    *   **Reward:** +10 Rep with BOTH factions.

17. **The Hybrid**
    *   **Giver:** Dr. V
    *   **Context:** A creature has been found that is half-flesh, half-circuit.
    *   **Objective:** Capture the `Cyber-Wolf` alive (use `Stun Baton`).
    *   **Reward:** `Wolf Companion` (Summon).

18. **Storm Chasing**
    *   **Giver:** The Meteorologist
    *   **Context:** We need readings from inside a Glass Storm.
    *   **Objective:** Place 3 `Sensors` while a storm is active.
    *   **Reward:** `Storm Cloak` (High resistance to storm damage).

---

## V. The Salt Hermits (The Deep Wastes)

19. **Salt Cure**
    *   **Giver:** Old Hagar
    *   **Context:** Her husband is turning to crystal too fast. She needs `Rock Salt`.
    *   **Objective:** Mine 5 `Salt Deposits` in the caves.
    *   **Reward:** `Salt Salve` (Cures 'Crystallizing' status).

20. **Hide and Seek**
    *   **Giver:** A Hermit Child
    *   **Context:** The child is bored.
    *   **Objective:** Find the child hiding in the ruins (Perception check).
    *   **Reward:** `Shiny Pebble` (It's useless, but cute) + `Secret Tunnel Location`.

21. **The Old Ways**
    *   **Giver:** The Elder
    *   **Context:** A pre-storm library is buried nearby.
    *   **Objective:** Retrieve a `Paper Book` (extremely rare).
    *   **Reward:** `Ancient Knowledge` (+Skill Point).

---

## VI. The Weird & The Wild (Global/Random)

22. **The Talking Rock**
    *   **Giver:** A Rock (Sector 7)
    *   **Context:** It's a rock. It's talking. It claims to be a god.
    *   **Objective:** Carry the rock to the "Highest Peak."
    *   **Reward:** The rock stops talking. (And you get +5 Strength).

23. **Ghost in the Shell**
    *   **Giver:** A malfunctioning vending machine.
    *   **Context:** The machine is dispensing grenades instead of soda.
    *   **Objective:** Fix the wiring or buy all the grenades.
    *   **Reward:** `Soda` or `Grenades`.

24. **Reflection's Request**
    *   **Giver:** Your own reflection in a mirror.
    *   **Context:** Your reflection moves when you don't. It asks for a favor.
    *   **Objective:** Break the mirror.
    *   **Reward:** `Shard of Self` (Unique Dagger).

25. **Shattered Dreams**
    *   **Giver:** A weeping Glassborn statue.
    *   **Context:** It cannot move, but it can project thoughts. It wants to see the ocean one last time.
    *   **Objective:** Describe the ocean to it (requires `Memory of the Sea` item).
    *   **Reward:** The statue shatters, revealing a `Pearl of Power`.

26. **The Glitch**
    *   **Giver:** The UI itself (Meta-Quest).
    *   **Context:** Text on the screen starts scrambling.
    *   **Objective:** Find the "Debug Terminal" in the world map.
    *   **Reward:** `Developer's Goggles` (See hidden walls).
