# Quest Structure & Endings

**Purpose:** To outline the narrative arc of the game, from the tutorial to the final choice.

---

## The Three-Act Structure

### Act 1: The Survivor (Levels 1-5)

- **Goal:** Survive the immediate dangers and reach the Nexus Plateau.
- **Key Quests:**
  - _Tutorial:_ "The First Storm" (Learn movement, combat, shelter).
  - _Main Quest:_ "The Signal" (Repair a radio tower to find the Nexus).
  - _Side Quests:_ "Water Run" (Hermits), "Scrap Hunt" (Engineers).
- **Climax:** Reaching the Nexus Plateau and meeting the Faction Representatives.

### Act 2: The Arbiter (Levels 5-15)

- **Goal:** Collect the Three Aspects of the Prime Lens.
- **Structure:** Non-linear. The player can tackle the three shards in any order.
  - _The Archive Heist:_ Infiltrate the Archive to get the **Shard of Clarity**. (Stealth/Hacking focus).
  - _The Magma Descent:_ Fight through the Iron Covenant mines to get the **Shard of Will**. (Combat focus).
  - _The Glass Pilgrimage:_ Navigate the Glass Gardens to treat with the High Prism for the **Shard of Soul**. (Diplomacy/Puzzle focus).
- **Faction Alignment:** During this act, the player builds reputation. High rep unlocks unique gear and shortcuts.

### Act 3: The Ascendant (Levels 15-20)

- **Goal:** Climb the Spire of Light and activate the Heliograph.
- **The Spire:** A final dungeon that tests all skills.
  - _Floor 1:_ Physical threats (Monsters).
  - _Floor 2:_ Puzzle threats (Light redirection).
  - _Floor 3:_ Existential threats (Data Ghosts / Boss Rush).
- **The Final Boss:** The "Guardian of the Spire" (A manifestation of the Heliograph's defense protocol).

---

## The Endings

When the player inserts the Prime Lens, they must choose a frequency.

### Ending A: Restoration (The Iron Path)

- **Action:** Activate the "Null-Pulse".
- **Result:**
  - The Storms stop forever.
  - All Glassborn die instantly (shatter).
  - Magic (Refraction) ceases to exist.
  - Humanity survives as purely biological, but the "Magic" of the world is gone.
- **Tone:** Melancholic Victory. Safety at the cost of wonder.

### Ending B: Ascension (The Glass Path)

- **Action:** Activate the "Full-Burn".
- **Result:**
  - The Storms consume the entire planet.
  - All biological life is vitrified.
  - Humanity ascends into a pure energy hive-mind within the glass.
  - Physical bodies are abandoned.
- **Tone:** Terrifying Transcendence. Evolution at the cost of humanity.

### Ending C: Synthesis (The Golden Path)

- **Action:** Tune the Lens to the "Harmony Frequency" (Requires high stats in both Tech and Magic).
- **Result:**
  - The Storms stabilize into a predictable cycle.
  - Humans and Glassborn coexist.
  - The Steppe becomes a lush, crystalline garden.
- **Tone:** Hopeful Balance. The hardest ending to achieve.

### Ending D: The Void (The Fail State)

- **Action:** Destroy the Lens / Fail the calibration.
- **Result:**
  - The Heliograph shuts down.
  - The sun goes dark.
  - The world freezes.
- **Tone:** Nihilism.
