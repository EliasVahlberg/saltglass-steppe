# The Lexicon of Refraction

*A guide to the dialects, slang, and idioms of the Saltglass Steppe.*

## 1. Common Tongue (Steppe-Speak)
Used by traders, hermits, and the unaligned. Practical, survival-focused.

*   **"Salt"**: Good, reliable, solid. *"He's salt."*
*   **"Glass"**: Dangerous, unstable, crazy. *"Don't go glass on me."*
*   **"Burned"**: Dead or corrupted.
*   **"The Hum"**: The ambient sound of the storm/magic.
*   **"Clear skies"**: Good luck / Safe travels.

## 2. Liturgy of the Mirror (Monk-Speak)
Used by the Mirror Monks and religious pilgrims. Metaphorical, formal, obsessed with light and clarity.

*   **"Reflect upon this"**: Think about this.
*   **"You are opaque"**: You are ignorant / You are hiding something.
*   **"May your angles be true"**: A blessing.
*   **"Fractured"**: Heretical / Broken / Insane.
*   **"The Great Prism"**: God / The Heliograph.

## 3. Tech-Cant (Engineer-Speak)
Used by the Iron Covenant and Sand-Engineers. Technical, cynical, precise.

*   **"Voltage"**: Power / Authority / Energy. *"High voltage situation."*
*   **"Null"**: No / Negative / Bad. *"That's a null plan."*
*   **"Glitch"**: A mutant / Glassborn / Magic-user.
*   **"Compile"**: To understand / To agree. *"Does that compile?"*
*   **"Format"**: To kill / To destroy. *"Format the target."*

## 4. The Choral Resonance (Glassborn-Speak)
Used by the Glassborn. Abstract, plural, poetic. Often refers to themselves as "We".

*   **"The Break"**: The moment of transformation.
*   **"Singing"**: Fighting / Using magic.
*   **"The Silence"**: Death / The Void.
*   **"Sharp"**: Good / Strong / Ready.
*   **"Dull"**: Weak / Human / Unchanged.

## Sample Dialogue Interactions

**Scenario: A Monk meets an Engineer.**

*   **Monk:** "The light reveals your path is fractured, builder."
*   **Engineer:** "Save the poetry. Your logic doesn't compile. We need voltage, not prayers."
*   **Monk:** "You are opaque to the truth. The Prism sees all."
*   **Engineer:** "The Prism is a machine. And machines break."

**Scenario: A Hermit meets a Glassborn.**

*   **Hermit:** "Stay back. I'm salt. I don't want trouble."
*   **Glassborn:** "We hear your hum, little grain. You are dull, but you vibrate with fear."
*   **Hermit:** "Just let me pass. I've got water."
*   **Glassborn:** "Water is silence. We thirst for the song."
