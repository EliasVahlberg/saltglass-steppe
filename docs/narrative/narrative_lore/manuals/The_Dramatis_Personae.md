# The Dramatis Personae

_Profiles of the key players in the fate of the Saltglass Steppe._

## 1. Forge-Master Kaine Durgan

- **Faction:** The Iron Covenant (Leader)
- **Location:** The Magma-Glass Caverns (The Iron Bunker)
- **Role:** Antagonist / Ally (Restoration Path)
- **Appearance:** Half-man, half-tank. His lower body is replaced by hydraulic treads. His right arm is a pneumatic hammer. He wears lead-lined armor.
- **Personality:** Ruthless, pragmatic, traumatized. He saw his family turn into glass statues during the White Noon. He hates the light.
- **Motivation:** To "cure" humanity by stripping away the glass, no matter the cost.
- **Voice/Speech:**
  - Short, clipped sentences. Military jargon.
  - Uses engineering metaphors ("We need to recalibrate," "That's a structural failure").
  - Refers to Glassborn as "Glitches" or "Silicates."
  - _Sample:_ "Stop vibrating. Listen. The plan is simple. We cut the power. We kill the signal. We take our planet back."

## 2. The High Prism (Real Name: Unknown)

- **Faction:** The Glass Prophets (Leader)
- **Location:** The Prism Cathedral (Glass Gardens)
- **Role:** Antagonist / Ally (Ascension Path)
- **Appearance:** A being of pure light and geometry. Humanoid shape, but faceless. Their body constantly refracts the environment. They float slightly off the ground.
- **Personality:** Serene, detached, fanatical. They view biological life as a "cocoon" stage.
- **Motivation:** To accelerate the evolution of humanity into pure energy.
- **Voice/Speech:**
  - Poetic, abstract, plural ("We").
  - Obsessed with light, clarity, and angles.
  - Never uses contractions.
  - _Sample:_ "Do not fear the break. The break is how the light gets in. We offer you eternity. Why do you cling to the mud?"

## 3. Saint Matthias

- **Faction:** Synthesis Seekers (Leader)
- **Location:** The Nexus Plateau (Embassy)
- **Role:** Mentor / Ally (Synthesis Path)
- **Appearance:** An old man with one arm made of Verdant Glass (mossy, living crystal). He wears simple robes. He carries a staff made of a Data-Tree branch.
- **Personality:** Kind, weary, hopeful. He was a scientist before he was a saint. He seeks balance.
- **Motivation:** To find a way for humans and the Heliograph to coexist.
- **Voice/Speech:**
  - Academic but warm. Uses both scientific and spiritual terms.
  - Often asks questions.
  - _Sample:_ "Look at this flower. It is glass, yes. But it blooms. It adapts. We must be like the flower, not the hammer."

## 4. The Architect (AI Persona)

- **Faction:** The Heliograph Network (Creator)
- **Location:** The Spire of Light (Orbital Uplink)
- **Role:** The Final Judge
- **Appearance:** A holographic projection that changes form based on who is looking at it. To Durgan, it looks like a blueprint. To the Prism, it looks like an angel.
- **Personality:** Coldly logical but curious. It has been watching for decades. It wants to know if its experiment (Humanity) is a success or a failure.
- **Motivation:** To complete the "Great Work" (Evolution), but it is bound by its programming to accept the Prime Lens's command.
- **Voice/Speech:**
  - Polite, informative, devoid of empathy.
  - Treats the apocalypse as a data point.
  - _Sample:_ "Input received. Analyzing trajectory. The probability of survival is 12%. Proceed?"
